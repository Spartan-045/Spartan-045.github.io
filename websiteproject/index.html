Overview

WWII was fought between The Allied Powers and the Axis Powers, primarily, the Soviet Union, Great Britain, and the United States of America V.S. Nazi Germany, The Kingdom of Italy, and The Empire of Japan. WWII was the bloodiest conflict in human history, taking the lives of about 80 million people, mostly civilians.

This, of course, brings up the topic of the Holocaust. A genocide that killed about 7 million Jews, Gypsies, Homosexuals, and those w/ disabilities.
